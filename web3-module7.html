Module 7 — DeFi (Extensive)

1. Quick Definition — What is DeFi?

Decentralized Finance (DeFi) means building financial services (lending, trading, staking, derivatives, insurance, etc.) on public blockchains so they run via smart contracts instead of centralized intermediaries.

2. The Core Primitives

  • Liquidity Pools (LPs): Smart contracts that hold reserves of tokens and let users trade against them.
  • Automated Market Makers (AMMs): Algorithms like Uniswap’s x*y=k that price trades automatically.
  • Lending & Borrowing: Protocols like Aave and Compound enable interest earning and borrowing with collateral.
  • Stablecoins: Tokens like DAI, USDC, USDT maintain stable values.
  • Staking & Liquid Staking: Lock tokens for network security and earn rewards; liquid staking gives a tradable token.

3. Common DeFi Actions

  • Swapping tokens on a DEX.
  • Providing liquidity and earning LP fees.
  • Lending, staking, borrowing, and yield farming.

4. Important Mechanics

  • AMM pricing: x*y=k formula sets pool prices.
  • Impermanent Loss: Loss LPs face when token price ratios change.
  • Flash Loans: Uncollateralized loans repaid in one transaction.

5. Real Protocols

Examples: Uniswap, Curve, Aave, Compound, MakerDAO, Lido.

6. Top Risks

Smart-contract bugs, impermanent loss, liquidation, oracle manipulation, flash-loan attacks, rug-pulls, bridge exploits.

7. Reducing Risk

Start small, use audited protocols, verify contracts, prefer multisig teams, use hardware wallets, monitor governance.

8. Advanced Topics

Yield aggregators, derivatives, DeFi insurance, composable strategies.

9. Getting Started Playbook

  1. Create a wallet (MetaMask + hardware for security).
  2. Fund with small amounts.
  3. Try swapping on Uniswap.
  4. Provide liquidity on testnets.
  5. Test Aave/Compound lending with tiny collateral.

10. Mini-Projects

  • Read-only LP explorer.
  • Testnet LP experiments.
  • Flash-loan sandbox study.

11. Resources

ConsenSys DeFi guides, Uniswap docs, Aave docs, Binance Academy, MakerDAO, Lido.

12. Recap

DeFi = open finance powered by code. Powerful, composable, but high risk.
